Creating a New Task:

To create a new task, click the "Create New Task" button at the top of the page. Fill in the title, description, and assignee's email. Click the "Create Task" button to add the task.

Show Completed Tasks:

You can toggle the display of completed tasks using the checkbox at the top. Checked means completed tasks will be shown, while unchecked means only active tasks are displayed.

Managing Tasks:

To mark a task as completed, simply click the checkbox next to it. Completed tasks will have a strikethrough effect.

To revert a task's completion status, click the checkbox again. The strikethrough effect will be removed, indicating the task is active again.

To edit a task, click on the edit icon. A dialog will pop up. Complete only the fields you want to modify.

To delete a task, click on the trash icon next to the checkbox. The task will be deleted permanently.

Getting Help:

If you have any questions or need assistance, feel free to click the "Click for help" button at the bottom of the page. This dialog will provide you with useful information and tips.

Thank you for using the Task Manager!
